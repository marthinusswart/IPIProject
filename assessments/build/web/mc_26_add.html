The Understanding

ADD Hyper-active children are normally very bright young people and understand things very quickly so they have to be managed differently from the average student; if there is such a students that can be classified or defined as average or normal?

We must understand the reality; that all people are uniquely different and everyone reacts to certain events differently in the same environment. However, the means by which children are managed is concerning, as we do not address the real cause of the problem. The problem did NOT originate with the child and usually it is NOT the child that is the problem.
