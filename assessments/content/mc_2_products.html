FQGlobal Products

Organizational Screening Inventory

The Organizational Screening Inventory is a profile of measurement that quantifies the functionality of the staff within that environment and to give the organization a true perspective on the true potential and functionality or efficiency of the organization within their present staff compliment.

Career Planner

The Career Planner is specifically designed to determine the career any individual should follow and or to scientifically prove whether or not your job profile and or the working environment is conducive to living a satisfied and fulfilled life-style.

Personal Profile

The Personal Profile is a scientific profile by which on can quantify the functionality of any individual within any specific environment, and define the different affects on the different systems and roles that the individual functions within.

Job Definition Match

Scientifically defines the criteria necessary within a job description; this definition will include the environmental effects of the job involved and then match it accordingly to the organizational screening inventory in order to get the ideal person in the right job. (right first time recruitment)
